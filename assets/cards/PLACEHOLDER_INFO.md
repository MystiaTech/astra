# Placeholder Card Images
=======================
DESIGN BY: Maya (Assets Lead)

This directory contains placeholder images for all 78 tarot cards.
These can be easily replaced with actual tarot deck artwork.

## Naming Convention

Cards follow this naming pattern:

### Major Arcana (0-21)
- `major_00_fool.png` - The Fool
- `major_01_magician.png` - The Magician
- `major_02_high_priestess.png` - The High Priestess
- ...
- `major_21_world.png` - The World

### Minor Arcana - Wands
- `wands_01_ace.png` - Ace of Wands
- `wands_02.png` through `wands_10.png` - Number cards
- `wands_11_page.png` - Page of Wands
- `wands_12_knight.png` - Knight of Wands
- `wands_13_queen.png` - Queen of Wands
- `wands_14_king.png` - King of Wands

## Recommended Image Specifications

- **Format:** PNG (transparent background preferred)
- **Size:** 300x500 pixels (card aspect ratio)
- **Resolution:** 72-150 DPI for web
- **Color Space:** RGB

## Placeholder Strategy

Current placeholders are:
1. Simple colored rectangles with card name text
2. Suit symbols for Minor Arcana
3. Roman numerals for Major Arcana

## Replacing Images

To replace with actual tarot artwork:

1. Acquire licensed tarot deck images OR
2. Commission original artwork OR
3. Use public domain decks (Rider-Waite-Smith variants)

Simply replace the PNG files following the naming convention above.
The bot will automatically use the new images.
